### **About**
Trust in the charity sector is declining due to scams and a lack of transparency regarding how funds are used. **Charity DAO** solves this by leveraging blockchain technology to ensure that every donation is traceable, immutable, and used for its intended purpose. 

This project implements a **Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO)** where donors receive Governance Tokens (GOV) in exchange for ETH contributions. Unlike traditional charities, funds are not released in a lump sum. Instead, we utilized a **milestone-based disbursement system** where NGOs must submit proof of work (uploaded to IPFS) which must be verified before the next tranche of funding is unlocked.

### **Governance & Architecture**
* **Quadratic Voting:** To prevent "whales" (wealthy donors) from dominating decision-making, we implemented a quadratic voting model where the cost of votes scales exponentially, giving smaller donors more proportional influence.
* **Reputation System:** Consistent participation is incentivized through a tiered reputation system that offers voting cost discounts to active, long-term donors.
* **Smart Contract Modules:**
    * **Treasury:** Manages ETH holdings and mints tokens.
    * **VotingManager:** Handles the logic for quadratic voting and reputation calculations.
    * **ProofOracle:** Verifies off-chain proofs (uploaded to Pinata) to trigger on-chain fund release.

### **Technologies Stack**
* **Smart Contracts:** Solidity (v0.8.24)
* **Security:** OpenZeppelin (AccessControl, ReentrancyGuard, Pausable)
* **Testing:** Hardhat, Ethers.js, Chai
* **Frontend:** React, Vite, TailwindCSS
* **Storage:** IPFS (via Pinata) for immutable proof storage
* **Wallet:** MetaMask Integration
